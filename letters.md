I am happy to write letters of recommendation for undergraduate students I have worked with. To help me write the strongest possible letter, please follow the guidelines below.

**Timing:** Please request letters at least four weeks before your first deadline.

**Eligibility:** While there is no strict GPA requirement, I am best positioned to write a strong, detailed letter for students who earned an A- or higher in my course, or who actively engaged through office hours and class participation.

## Required Materials

Please send one email containing all of the following:

- List of courses taken with me (include semester and year)
- Current resume or CV
- Draft personal statement or statement of purpose
- A brief summary of two or three specific projects, contributions, or moments from class that you would like me to highlight
- Clear deadline list for all programs you are applying to

## A Note on GSIs

For large lecture courses, I may consult the graduate student instructor who worked with you. I may ask them to provide specific feedback or a draft to ensure the letter is as comprehensive as possible.

## How to Submit

Email all materials to [susanhyde@berkeley.edu](mailto:susanhyde@berkeley.edu) with the subject line:

**"Letter of Recommendation Request - [Your Name]"**
